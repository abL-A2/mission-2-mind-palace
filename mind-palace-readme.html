mind.palace


mind.palace is based on the mnemonic device of visualising one's memories as being kept inside distinct locations within a 'mental palace', with the act of recalling memories as traversing space within that palace to visit or take from those locations.

This app attempts to create a stripped-down digital framework of that mnemonic device, packaged in a hybrid theme of programming syntax and the metaphor itself: a palace with halls leading to an ever-expanding labyrinth of rooms.
