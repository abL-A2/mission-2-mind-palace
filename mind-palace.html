  • about mind.palace

mind.palace.build();

one word, only letters. no spaces, numbers, special chars - broad category or class. this becomes a HALL. multiple rooms can be in a hall. halls must be unique. if inputting an existing hall, will add room and door to that hall instead of creating new hall.

sentences, small paragraph, max 500 chars - content. this becomes a ROOM. a room must have a door, and must belong to a hall.

0/500

one word, only letters. no spaces, numbers, special chars - the subject line of your room. this becomes its DOOR. a door must have a room, and is used for finding the room within a hall without searching through the room.

create a new HALL or access an existing one, build a new ROOM into that hall, and close that room with a DOOR. review inputs, then click .build()

mind.palace.find();

always start with '>'. use >all to retrieve full list of built halls. use >(hallname) to retrieve a specific hall and list all its rooms, which you can enter by clicking its 'enter room' button. use >(hallname)-(doorname) to retrieve a single door and immediately enter.
